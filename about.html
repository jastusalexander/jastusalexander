An event planner is a professional or company specializing in organizing and executing various types of events, from weddings to corporate gatherings.

They handle everything from budgeting and vendor coordination to logistics and guest experience, ensuring a seamless and memorable event for their clients. Event planners are experts in creativity, problem-solving, and attention to detail, making them essential for successful events.
